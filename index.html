About Donnie Fryman, CPA, PSC

Donnie Fryman has been providing professional accounting services in Georgetown, KY since 2007. Throughout his years as a partner with Welch & Company CPAs, Donnie has built an excellent reputation within the community for his hard work and fast, friendly service. Donnie graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting from Georgetown University in 2007 and became a Certified Public Accountant in April 2011. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Kentucky Society of CPAs (KyCPA).

We take pride in building long, personable relationships with our clients so we can be your trusted partner for success.
